Recommended Actions by Team
Product / Ops Monitor delivery drops, submitted statuses, and RNA spikes. Set up automated threshold alerts for gateway failures and response-not-answered rates exceeding 70%.
Account Manager Prioritize inactive high-value users (esp. Client B Retail) and mono-channel users (Client A Finance). Offer bundled multi-channel packages to reduce churn risk.
Campaign Ops Use peak day/hour patterns (Mon–Fri, 10:00–14:00) to optimize campaign timing. Avoid scheduling high-volume SMS/WhatsApp blasts during off-peak windows with low server capacity.
